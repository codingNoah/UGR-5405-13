Ever Hills Hotel

Ever Hills is located in Bole, just a 20-minute walk from the Bole International AirLines. Built in 2022 it has old school charm and many rooms still feature high, crown-molded ceilings. A 2012 renovation brought all rooms and services up to modern day scratch and guestrooms come equipped with free Wi-Fi and all the usual amenities required for a comfortable stay. Program Leader Evaluations: “This is a great location. Good service and nice breakfast.”

Dining Tables

At Ever Hills, we’re serving up more than 40 types of foods. In fact, Ever Hills Famous on local Foods, Shiro is one of our unexpected specialties. Reminiscent of butcher shops back in the day, each slow-smoked, sizzling prime chop measures seven-fingers high. Our signature recipe, that we have perfected for more than one year, is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings, cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it’s topped with Ever Hills signature herb-garlic butter, then carved tableside.
